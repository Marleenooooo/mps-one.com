# Glossary

- PR (Purchase Request): internal request to procure items/services; requires approval.
- Quote: supplier proposal with items/prices/taxes/validity; supports versioning.
- PO (Purchase Order): contract issued from an accepted quote; defines terms and quantities.
- Delivery Letter (Delivery Note): document per shipment with quantities, tracking, dates; confirmed by client.
- Invoice: billing document based on confirmed deliveries; can be partial.
- Payment: recorded against an invoice with amount/date/method.
- PIC: Person In Charge for a department or function.
- Audit Trail: record of actions, actors, timestamps, and comments.
